The Doctor Appointment System is an online healthcare platform that enables patients to conveniently book appointments with doctors.
Users can browse a list of available doctors, register or log in, and select their desired physician.
After selecting a doctor, they can choose an appointment time slot. The system sends a confirmation email to the patient once the appointment is booked.
Additionally, patients have access to their appointment history for reference. 
The platform simplifies the appointment scheduling process, providing an efficient and user-friendly healthcare solution
